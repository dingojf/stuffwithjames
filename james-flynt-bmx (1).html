About Me

WHO IS
JAMES?

🏁 #70 · Chehalem Valley BMX

Hey! I'm James Flynt — a kid from Dundee, Oregon who lives and breathes BMX. From the moment I wake up to the moment I crash into bed (sometimes literally), bikes are basically my whole world.


I race at Chehalem Valley BMX, and there's nothing quite like it. The rush of dropping off the starting gate, pedaling full speed through the rhythm sections, flying over jumps, and throwing everything you have at the finish line — it's an addiction, and I'm totally okay with that.


When I'm not on the race track, you'll find me at the skatepark riding BMX freestyle and practicing tricks. I also ride scooters with friends, go to Forge: An Acton Academy, and run my YouTube channel James Flynt BMX with 385 subscribers and growing!

🏁
BMX Racing

Racing at Chehalem Valley BMX is where my passion was born. The starting hill, the tight corners, the massive jumps — every race is a new challenge and a new opportunity to push my limits on the track.

🛹
Skatepark Life

The skatepark is my second home. Whether it's hitting ramps on the BMX or pulling tricks on a scooter with my crew, the park is where creativity meets grit. Always something new to try.

🎒
Forge Acton Academy

School doesn't have to be boring. At Forge, we learn by doing — real projects, real problem solving, real skills. It's a totally different kind of education that actually prepares you for the real world.

🎬
YouTube Creator

I run a YouTube channel called James Flynt BMX with 385 subscribers and growing. I post riding videos, skatepark sessions, and behind-the-scenes content. The grind never stops!

The Track

WHERE I
RIDE

Chehalem Valley BMX is my home track, and it's where I go to compete, train, and improve. BMX racing is a sport that rewards commitment — the more you put in, the more you get out. I love the strategy involved: how you pick your line, time your pedal strokes, and battle wheel-to-wheel with other riders all the way to the line.


I'm registered on USA BMX, the national governing body for BMX racing in the US. Check out my official profile through the links below!
